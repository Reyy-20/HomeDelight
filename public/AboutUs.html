Meet Home Delight

Home Delight makes finding a home simple, secure, and personal. With expert guidance and 24/7 support from our virtual assistant Stella, we help you move from searching to living—smoothly and confidently.

Because a home is more than a place—it's where your story begins.

Our story

Home Delight was born from the vision of students who believed homes deserve more than just walls and roofs. Since 2024, we've been transforming house hunting into a simple, personalized experience—combining smart technology with expert guidance. Our purpose is to give every home that "something special" that makes it unique: comfort, personality, and joy.

Vision

We strive to be the leading home care app, connecting communities with innovation and sustainability, empowering people to find their perfect home.

Mission

Our mission is to guide families in finding their ideal home with personalized support, making the process simple and stress-free.

Our Values

  • Commitment
  • Empathy
  • Accessibility
  • Transparency

These values will be reflected in our website while you enjoy your incredible search, offering an environment of commitment.
